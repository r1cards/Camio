Why did we chose these specific scenarios?

Outdoor

In the exterior photos we can fully appreciate the dynamic range of the cameras sensor, as well as the details in the edges of the objects.

Indoor

The interior photos are made with artificial lights that are not always bright, so that we are able to see how much noise is present aswell as the color fidelity in these conditions.

Low light

In the exterior photos with very low light we can appreciate the ability of the camera sensor to capture images without noise in these extreme lowlight conditions.

Flash

In photos taken with flash we should look at the power and brightness of the light and if it is able to capture a well detailed images.

Selfie

In these images we are able to see the capability of the front camera sensor when taking pictures of a person at a hand held distance.
